About DoctorLink

Our Mission

DoctorLink aims to revolutionize access to healthcare across Africa. By leveraging modern Web2 technologies and secure, decentralized Web3 solutions, we are building a gig-economy platform that bridges the gap between healthcare providers and patients in underserved regions.

The Challenge

In many parts of South Africa and the broader continent, patients face significant barriers to quality healthcare, including long travel times, limited specialist availability, and opaque, high out-of-pocket costs. Our platform simplifies this process, providing instant, trusted, and transparent access.

Our Solution

DoctorLink empowers healthcare professionals to offer consultations on their own terms, while providing patients with a mobile-first, user-friendly interface to manage their health. By utilizing blockchain for secure escrow and smart-contract-based payments, we ensure that both doctors and patients are protected in every transaction.
